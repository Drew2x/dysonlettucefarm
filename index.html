New Technologies

• DySon Lettuce Farm is one of the emerging lettuce farm using hydrophonics method in farming

Hydrophonic farming is a way of growing plants that substitutes water for soil. Instead of planting crops in the ground or even in raised beds filled with soil, hydroponic produce is grown with roots dangling in water.

• This lettuce farm conserves water and has proper filtration and treatment allows the water to remain clean.

Water conservation is a primary concern in today’s world. It may seem counterintuitive to replace soil with water in a hydroponic system.
